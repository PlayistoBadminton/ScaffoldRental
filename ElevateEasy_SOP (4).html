Damage / safety incident Home
What type of incident?
Select the situation
Equipment damage
  • Ask customer to send clear photos on WhatsApp immediately
  • Do NOT quote any repair or deduction amount to customer
  • Call owner and share the photos right away
  • Log: customer name, date reported, description of damage
  • Owner will decide deduction from security deposit
Person injured — act immediately
  • Step 1: Call emergency services if needed — 108 (ambulance)
  • Step 2: Call owner immediately — share location and situation
  • Step 3: Do not move the scaffold until owner or authority arrives
  • Step 4: Photograph the scene — scaffold position, surroundings
  • Step 5: Get injured person's name and contact; do not admit fault
Scaffold collapsed or unsafe
  • Step 1: Ensure no one is on or near the scaffold
  • Step 2: Call owner immediately — do not attempt re-assembly alone
  • Step 3: Photograph collapsed scaffold before touching anything
  • Step 4: Cordon off the area — keep bystanders away
  • Step 5: Wait for owner's instruction before any further action
